The comparison with Cross transformer

This section shows the generated audio from 4 different configurations.
(1) One stage (using only κ=1 codec layers)
(1-1)Prefix SiMBA
(1-2)Cross Transformer
(2) Two stage
(2-1)Prefix SiMBA + LDM
(2-2)Cross Transformer + LDM
We train the Prefix SiMBA and Cross Transformer in (1) with the same training data and same training steps, and then use the trained model to generate rudimentary audio samples. And further use the generated results from (1) passing to the second stage model, LDM, to generate intact audio samples.
The generated audio samples from (1) are rudimentary, which means those are reconstructed by only the first layer of the codec(κ=1).
The generated audio samples from (2) are intact audio samples coresponding to the full codec(κ=9).
The results of (1-2) and (2-2) are not shown in the paper and the previous demo page, because the audio quality is not good enough to be presented. However, to response the comments from reviewers, we still include the generated results here with a comparison table for completeness.
